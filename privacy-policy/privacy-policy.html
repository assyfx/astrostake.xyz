Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the website AstroStake.xyz, including its subdomains docs.astrostake.xyz and vault.astrostake.xyz ("we", "our", "AstroStake").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from users:

  • IP Address: Collected when users send a message via the contact form, submit an RPC on the monitoring page, or request faucet access.
  • Wallet Address: Collected when requesting faucet access, stored temporarily for 7 days.
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics and Cloudflare to monitor website performance and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Contact Form: To respond to user inquiries sent through the form.
  • RPC Monitoring: To validate and monitor RPC submissions.
  • Faucet Requests: To ensure fair usage and prevent abuse, with data automatically deleted after 7 days.
  • Analytics: To analyze website traffic and user interactions using Google Analytics and Cloudflare.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties except as required by law or to maintain the security of our services.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Our website uses HTTPS (Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure) to encrypt the communication between your device and our servers. This helps protect your data from interception or unauthorized access.

We also implement security measures, such as access controls and secure data storage, to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of your information.

5. Data Retention

IP addresses and wallet addresses are retained for a maximum of 7 days for faucet requests. Analytics data is retained according to the policies of Google Analytics and Cloudflare.

6. User Rights

Users may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data.

7. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Please note that we do not have control over the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

8. Data Transfer and Jurisdiction

Your data may be processed or stored on servers located in Germany, subject to the data protection regulations of the European Union (GDPR). However, our primary jurisdiction is Indonesia, and this Privacy Policy is governed by Indonesian law.

If you are located in the European Union, you have certain rights under GDPR, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data.

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact form on our main website.
